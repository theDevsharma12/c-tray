What is a Digital Product Passport?

Your Product’s Digital DNA: From Raw Materials to Recycling

A Digital Product Passport (DPP) is a blockchain-secured digital record that stores a product’s lifecycle data—material composition, carbon footprint, compliance status, and recycling instructions—enabling transparency and regulatory readiness.
